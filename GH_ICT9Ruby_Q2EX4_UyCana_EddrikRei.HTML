Mathematics

Improve your math, problem-solving, and strategic competence with the fundamentals of mathematics.
This concise book is intended for students from all backgrounds and aspiring math majors. By focusing on a particular number of fundamental notions, the text digs thoroughly enough into each subject to both challenge students and provide real world applications.












Sciences

Improve decision-making, problem-solving, and invention with brain training.
Think Smarter: Critical Thinking to Improve Problem-Solving and Decision-Making Skills is the all-inclusive book for teaching your brain to work harder for you. The book, which was written by a critical thinking trainer and coach, offers a practical set of tools for using critical thinking methods to solve common business problems. Think Smarter is a useful tool for people, managers, students, and corporate training programs since it is brimming with real-world examples that show how the tools are used in reality, as well as hundreds of practice activities relevant across sectors and roles.
